About realkey

realkey is a browser-native CAD workspace for generating precise, fabrication-ready keys and locksport tools. Geometry is built locally on your device.

realkey is powered at its core by build123d, a code-based CAD system written in Python.

Many target locks have very tight tolerances. The following settings are a practical starting point for print quality.

Recommended 3D printing settings

Good key materials are rigid and wear resistant. If a material is suitable for gears, it may be a useful starting point.

Materials

  • Carbon fiber nylon
  • Polycarbonate
  • PETG
  • PLA — usable, but comparatively brittle

Process settings

  • Nozzle0.4 mm
  • Layer height0.08 mm
  • First layer0.16–0.20 mm
  • Top line width0.32 mm
  • Wall orderOuter / Inner
  • Wall loops3
  • Top shell layers9
  • Infill100% gyroid
  • Outer wall50 mm/s
  • Inner wall80 mm/s
  • SupportsEnabled
  • Top Z distance0.16–0.20 mm
